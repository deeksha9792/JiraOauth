Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our Jira integration application (“Application”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users connect their Atlassian Jira workspace to our services.

1. Information We Access

When a user authorizes our application through Atlassian OAuth, we may access certain Jira workspace information based on the permissions granted by the user.

This may include:

  • Jira project metadata
  • Issue and ticket information
  • Project activity events
  • User and workspace identifiers
  • Webhook event data from Jira

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to provide project analytics, automation, insights, reporting, and integration functionality.

Our application may automatically:

  • Create webhooks within authorized Jira workspaces
  • Receive Jira event notifications
  • Analyze project activity and operational metrics
  • Generate insights and workflow recommendations

3. OAuth Tokens and Security

OAuth access tokens and related credentials are stored securely using industry-standard security practices.

We take reasonable measures to protect data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade customer data to third parties.

Information accessed through Jira is used only for providing functionality related to this application.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide integration services and comply with operational or legal obligations.

Users may revoke access to the application at any time through Atlassian account settings.

6. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with Atlassian Jira Cloud APIs and related Atlassian services.

Use of Atlassian services is subject to Atlassian’s own terms and privacy policies.

7. User Rights

Users may request removal of stored integration data or revoke workspace access at any time.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updated versions will be published on this page.
